SERIVCES

Residental Level of Care

At this level of care, clients get time to get stabilized on medications, they also have 24/7 staff available and clinicians available during the week. They will also receive weekly sessions with there therapist as well as with their case manager.

Outpatient Level of Care

At this level of care, they will receive weekly groups with their peers, support as they transition to sober living and continue having weekly sessions with a therapist and case manager.
